Analyze and improve the branding and visual identity of this project.

1. BRAND CONSISTENCY AUDIT — Identify every place the brand appears: logo, favicons, OG images, email templates, error pages, loading screens. Is the logo used consistently (same version, same clear space, no stretching)?

2. BRAND VOICE — Review all text in the app (headlines, CTAs, error messages, empty states, onboarding). Is the tone consistent? Identify the brand voice (professional/casual/playful/authoritative) and flag any text that breaks it.

3. VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM — Does the project have: (1) a logo in SVG format, (2) a defined color palette, (3) a typography system, (4) an icon style, (5) a photography/illustration direction? List what is missing.

4. FAVICON & META IMAGES — Is there a favicon.ico and apple-touch-icon.png? Is there an og:image (1200×630px) for social sharing? Is the og:image branded and informative (not just the logo on white)?

5. BRAND DIFFERENTIATION — How does this brand visually differentiate from competitors? Identify 2-3 direct competitors and compare: color (similar?), typography (similar?), layout style (similar?). Suggest one bold visual decision that would make this brand more distinctive.

6. LOADING & EMPTY STATES — Are the loading screen and empty states (no results, no data, first-time use) branded? They are high-visibility moments for brand expression. Suggest improvements.

Provide a brand audit report and specific recommendations for the most impactful improvements.
